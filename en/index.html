The Problem

The challenges of traditional renewable energy development

Renewable energy project development is still a long, complex and expensive process. Traditional methods require years of manual analysis and present high error risks.

Long Development Times

Developing a renewable energy project requires an average of 2-3 years, from site research to obtaining permits, due to manual processes and repetitive analysis.

Regulatory Complexity

Regulations change continuously at regional and national levels. Staying updated and compliant requires specialized expertise and constant monitoring.

High Costs

Feasibility studies, environmental analysis, and specialized consultancy represent significant costs, often without guarantee of project success.

Limited Territorial Analysis

Identifying suitable areas is based on limited manual analysis, missing hidden opportunities and not optimizing the territory's energy potential.

AI Solutions

Advanced technologies for energy development

We combine computer vision, large language models and big data analysis to automate the identification of suitable areas and navigation of the complex regulatory landscape.

Computer Vision

Our vision models automatically analyze satellite data, cadastral maps and territorial constraints to identify areas with the highest energy potential. The algorithm processes thousands of geographical, climatic and regulatory parameters in real time.

Large Language Models

We use large language models specifically trained to interpret and monitor Italian and European energy regulations. The system continuously analyzes legislative updates, environmental constraints and authorization procedures, always ensuring maximum regulatory compliance.

Big Data Analytics

Our platform integrates and analyzes enormous datasets from cadastral, environmental, urban planning and regulatory sources. Through advanced machine learning algorithms, we transform bureaucratic and territorial complexity into actionable insights and data-driven decisions to accelerate renewable energy project development.
